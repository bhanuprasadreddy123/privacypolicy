Privacy Policy - Anger Management

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to Anger Management, your comprehensive emotional wellness and anger management companion. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information and emotional health data.

This Privacy Policy outlines:

  • What data we collect and how we use it.
  • How we store and secure your information.
  • Your rights regarding your data.
  • How to contact us for concerns or inquiries.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name and username
  • Phone number (used for OTP authentication via Firebase)
  • Email address (for account recovery and communication)
  • Date of birth and age
  • Gender (for personalized recommendations)
  • Profile picture (optional, stored locally or on Firebase Storage)

1.2 Emotional Wellness Data

  • Mood tracking entries and emotional journal entries
  • Anger triggers and patterns you record
  • Coping strategies and techniques used
  • Progress tracking data and session history
  • Meditation session records (duration, type, timestamps)
  • Breathing exercise completion data
  • Emotional insights and self-assessment results
  • Goals and achievements

1.3 Face Detection Data

Our app uses Google ML Kit Face Detection for emotional analysis:

  • Facial images are processed locally on your device
  • No facial images are stored or transmitted to our servers
  • Only emotion analysis results (mood data) are saved to your profile
  • Camera permissions are required but images are not retained

1.4 Device and Usage Data

  • IP address and device information
  • App usage statistics and feature interactions
  • Session duration and frequency of app usage
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • Activity timestamps
  • Device storage access (for profile pictures and media)

1.5 Media and Files

We may access your camera and storage to:

  • Capture photos for face detection and emotional analysis
  • Select profile pictures from your device gallery
  • Store and manage app-related media files locally

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate users securely via Firebase Authentication (phone OTP and email/password).
  • Provide personalized anger management techniques and recommendations.
  • Track your emotional wellness progress and identify patterns.
  • Generate insights based on your mood trends and triggers.
  • Enable face detection for real-time emotional analysis.
  • Store meditation and breathing exercise session data.
  • Maintain your health history, progress dashboard, and profile.
  • Send reminders, motivational messages, and notifications.
  • Improve app performance and user experience through analytics.
  • Enable ambient sound playback and personalized calm space features.

3. Data Security

Security measures include:

  • Firebase Authentication with secure OTP verification
  • HTTPS encrypted communication for all data transmission
  • Firebase Realtime Database and Firestore with access controls
  • Secure cloud storage (Firebase Storage) with encryption
  • Local data processing for sensitive face detection (no cloud uploads)
  • Secure file handling and temporary storage
  • Regular security updates and audits
  • User-based data access rules (users can only access their own data)

4. Emotional Health Data Protection

Special protections for emotional health information:

  • Emotional and mood data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Face detection processing occurs locally on your device (no image storage)
  • Journal entries and personal reflections are stored securely
  • Progress data is accessible only to you through your authenticated account
  • User control over data retention and deletion
  • No sharing of emotional data with third parties without consent

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • View and update your profile information at any time
  • Access your mood tracking history and progress data
  • Request data correction or modification
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your journal entries and progress data
  • Opt out of notifications and promotional communications
  • Revoke camera or storage permissions through device settings
  • Control what data is shared and stored

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Realtime Database, Firestore, Storage, and Analytics for core app functionality
  • Google ML Kit: Face detection services (processes images locally on your device, no data sent to Google)
  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance

All third-party services are required to handle your data securely and responsibly according to industry standards. Firebase and Google ML Kit comply with Google's privacy policies and data protection measures.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • Profile information: Until you delete your account
  • Mood tracking and emotional data: As long as you maintain an active account
  • Meditation and breathing session records: Stored in Firebase until account deletion
  • Journal entries and notes: Until manually deleted by you or upon account deletion
  • Progress tracking data: Retained to maintain your progress history
  • Analytics data: Anonymized and aggregated, retained for service improvement

When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required for legal compliance or anonymized analytics.

8. Children's Privacy

Anger Management is not designed for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States where Firebase services are hosted. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data, including encryption and compliance with data protection regulations.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. All changes will be reflected in this document and on our website. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when the most recent changes were made.
